DIFFERENCE BETWEEN PADDINGS, MARGINS AND BORDERS
  • Padding is the space between an element's border and the element's content. It controls the space inside an element. They're used when you’re adjusting the look of an individual element
  • Margin is the space around an element's border. It controls the space outside an element. They're used when you’re adjusting the spacing of an element in relation to another element
  • Border is drawn between the margin and the padding of a box. It surrounds the padding and content
